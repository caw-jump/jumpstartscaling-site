## Paying Humans For Robot Work

In 2026, forcing a $25/hour employee to manually transfer leads from Facebook to a spreadsheet is financial negligence.

### How to use this calculator
1. Count exactly how many employees perform the identical repetitive task (e.g., SDRs sending prospect emails, Support staff categorizing tickets).
2. Estimate the total hours per day they spend doing this task globally.
3. Enter their average hourly wage.
4. Set an AI Efficiency target (80% means AI handles the bulk, humans quickly approve).

### Reallocating Payroll to Revenue
This tool visualizes **Burn Rate** vs **Recaptured Value**. When you save an employee 15 hours a week, you aren't just saving the wage cost of those 15 hours. You are granting them 15 *new* hours to execute high-value strategy that generates top-line revenue. Automation compounds.
