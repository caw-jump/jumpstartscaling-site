## What is the average cost to replace a roof in 2026?

As of 2026, the national average to replace an asphalt shingle roof on a standard 2,000 sq ft home is between **$9,000 and $13,000**. However, upgrading to premium materials like Standing Seam Metal or Slate can push the replacement cost between $18,000 and $45,000+.

### How to use this calculator
1. Enter your estimated roof square footage.
2. Select your desired roofing material (Asphalt, Metal, Tile, or Slate).
3. Select the steepness of your roof.
4. Input the number of old layers that need to be torn off.
