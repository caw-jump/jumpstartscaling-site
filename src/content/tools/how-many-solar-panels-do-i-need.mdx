## Sizing Your Solar Array

Solar installers don't size systems based on your home's square footage—they size it purely based on your kWh consumption and your geographic sunlight exposure.

### How to use this calculator
1. Enter your average monthly electric bill.
2. Provide your utility's Cost per kWh (found on your bill, usually between $0.12 and $0.25).
3. Select the peak sun hours for your region (Southwest gets more sun than the Northeast).
4. Select the wattage of the panels you are buying (400W is the modern standard).
