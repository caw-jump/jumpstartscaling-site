## Are Google Ads Too Expensive?

Google Search intent is incredibly high—people searching for "emergency AC repair" have their credit cards out. That's why the Cost Per Click (CPC) in local service niches is astronomically high, sometimes hitting $50 or $100 per click.

### How to use this calculator
1. Input your monthly ad budget (e.g. $3000 to start).
2. Use Google Keyword Planner to find your geographic CPC.
3. Input your **Website Conversion Rate**. If you send traffic to a standard homepage with 10 menus and distractions, use 3%. If you use an aggressively optimized funnel, use 10-15%.
4. Enter your sales team's close rate from organic/inbound leads.

### The Math Dictates The Winner 
If your business can convert heavily, you can afford to pay more per click than your competitors. He who can afford to pay the most to acquire a customer, wins. See the true math before pulling the trigger.
