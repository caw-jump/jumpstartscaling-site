## The Field Service Match

For local service businesses (HVAC, Roofing, Plumbing), marketing doesn't stop at the website. The true pipeline requires operations to convert the lead:

`ROI = [ (Leads * Booking Rate * Tech Close Rate * Avg Ticket) - Ad Spend ] / Ad Spend * 100`

### How it's used
Instead of just measuring cost-per-click from Google Ads or Facebook, operators input their unique operational metrics: the efficiency of the dispatch office (booking rate), the specific technician's close rate in the home, and the average invoice size for that specific service.

### Why it matters
It connects front-end marketing directly to dispatch logistics. A $150 lead looks extremely expensive to a media buyer. But if the technician closes at 40% and the average ticket is $12,000, this calculator proves the campaign is highly profitable and should be aggressively scaled, bypassing surface-level vanity metrics.
