## Monetizing the SERPs

SEO agencies shouldn't sell "rankings"; they should sell projected revenue. This calculator connects top-of-funnel search volume directly to your bottom line.

`Monthly Value = Search Volume * CTR * Conversion Rate * Customer Value`

### How it's used
Use Ahrefs or SEMRush to find the Monthly Search Volume of a target keyword. Then, input a realistic Click-Through Rate assumption, your site's current organic conversion rate, and your Customer Lifetime Value (or Average Order Value).

### Why it matters
It allows you to ruthlessly prioritize your content calendar. You might find that ranking #3 for a highly-targeted 'bottom-of-funnel' keyword with only 200 monthly searches generates exponentially more revenue than ranking #1 for a broad, informational keyword with 5,000 searches. Content is expensive—write the articles that actually generate cash.
