## The Zapier Extortion Model

Zapier is brilliant for connecting two apps quickly when you're a startup. But the moment you scale—and your CRM data requires complex branching, looping, or mass updating—Zapier's task-based pricing model exponentially drains your margin. 

### How to use this calculator
1. Check your Zapier Dashboard for your **monthly task usage** (or execution limit).
2. Enter that number on the sliding scale. 
3. The calculator pits standard Zapier SaaS tiers against the raw server cost of hosting an open-source `n8n` droplet on AWS or DigitalOcean.

### The Power of Ownership
When you hit 100,000 tasks a month (which is extremely easy to hit when routing webhook data), Zapier charges roughly **$700 a month**. A self-hosted instance of n8n requires a $20 Linux droplet. In one year, that $680/mo discrepancy pays for a full custom-migration and puts $8,000 back into your bank account. Stop renting your infrastructure.
