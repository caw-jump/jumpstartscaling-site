## Finding the Bottleneck

This tool mathematically isolates net campaign profitability by subtracting operational costs from gross margin across the entire funnel timeline.

`Net Profit = (Traffic * ConvRate * LeadToOpp * OppToClose * AvgDealValue * Margin) - Spend`

### How it's used
You break down the conversion rates at every single micro-step from website visitor to closed deal. Rather than just picking an arbitrary "Cost per Lead" target, you map the reality of your sales team's closing ability.

### Why it matters
It isolates exactly where a campaign is failing. If traffic converts to leads at 10% (great landing page), but leads become qualified opportunities at only 2%, it highlights a severe lead quality or initial follow-up issue. It mathematically proves that pouring more ad spend into the top of the funnel won't fix a broken SDR bottleneck.
