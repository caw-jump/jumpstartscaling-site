## The Appointment Setting Waterfall

In outbound marketing, results strictly trace the waterfall of contact, engagement, and conversion drop-offs.

`Booked = Outbound Volume * Connect Rate * Meeting Set Rate`

`Closed Won = Booked * Show Rate * Close Rate * Deal Size`

### How it's used
You input your cold total outreach (SMS, cold emails, voicemails) and estimate the percentage drop-off rate at each critical stage: Connects, Books, Shows, and Closes.

### Why it matters
It visually proves the compounding power of optimizing your follow-up sequence. By playing with the calculator, you can mathematically prove that improving your appointment show-up rate by just 5% (perhaps through an automated SMS reminder sequence) drastically increases bottom-line revenue—without spending an extra dime acquiring net-new leads at the top of the funnel.
