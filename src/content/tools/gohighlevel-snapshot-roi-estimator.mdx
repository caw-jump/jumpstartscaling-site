## Escape the Unscalable Agency Hamster Wheel

Traditional marketing agencies often implode when they scale because high-ticket custom fulfillment breaks down. Moving away from custom 1-to-1 labor and into a 1-to-Many software (SaaS) model stabilizes your revenue floor.

### How to use this calculator
1. Select the number of clients you believe you can sell a software snapshot to.
2. Enter the pricing tier you intend to run (usually $97 for basic lead gen, $297 for automation, $497 for full AI integration).
3. Select your telecom markup if you intend to profit off of their SMS marketing volume constraints. 

Because the central software cost is completely fixed, every single client past client #2 or #3 is pure 100% margin that drops straight to your agency's bottom line.
