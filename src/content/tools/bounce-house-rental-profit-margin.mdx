## The Economics of Inflatables

A $3,000 water slide looks expensive until you realize it can generate $10,000 a year for 5 consecutive years. The key to operating a lucrative event rental company is strict inventory ROI monitoring. 

### How to use this calculator
1. Enter the total purchase cost of the new bounce house, slide, or tent (including shipping/freight).
2. Enter the gross price you charge the customer for a standard rental.
3. Factor in the hard costs. You must deduct the cost of gas, the labor rate for the driver setting it up, and the wear-and-tear insurance. **Do not pretend labor is free just because you drive the truck yourself—your time has a wage.**

This algorithm strips out the fantasy and tells you exactly how many weekends you must be booked to own the asset free and clear.
