## Forecasting the Funnel

High-ticket deals require a specific breakdown focusing on continuous Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) relative to initial Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC). 

`LTV:CAC = (Avg Contract Value * Gross Margin) / (Total Sales & Marketing Spend / New Customers)`

### How it's used
You input the length of your sales cycle (e.g., 90 days) alongside your marketing spend. It forecasts true net revenue and CAC, adjusting for gross fulfillment margin.

### Why it matters
High-ticket B2B or commercial service sales have delayed attribution. If you spend $10,000 in January and get zero closed deals that month, standard eCommerce calculators show a -100% ROI. This calculator accounts for the pipeline delay, projecting how that January spend will actually yield a 400%+ ROI by April when the pipeline finally signs the contracts.
